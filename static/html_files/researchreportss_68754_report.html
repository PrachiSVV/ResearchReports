DRChoksey IPO Note on ArisInfra Solutions Ltd

Published Date: 2025-06-17

Source: Deven Choksey

Company Name

ArisInfra Solutions Ltd

Sector

Technology-enabled B2B construction material procurement platform

Analyst View

Recommend 'AVOID' due to high IPO valuation at 48.3x FY25 EV/EBITDA and thin operating margins despite integrated operations and tech-enabled model. Profitability improvement appears limited given reliance on B2B segment and short-term borrowings.

Business Summary

ArisInfra Solutions Ltd is a technology-enabled B2B platform sourcing and delivering a wide range of construction materials across 18 states in India. It leverages digital tools to offer an integrated, asset-light procurement model supported by a vast vendor network, serving infrastructure and real estate projects.

Industry Overview

The Indian construction materials market is large and fragmented, with infrastructure and real estate B2B segments valued at USD 275-295bn in 2024. The market is characterized by inefficiencies like supply inconsistency, lack of pricing transparency, and fragmented vendor networks. Government investments and initiatives are driving infrastructure growth. Digital platforms like ArisInfra play a key role in streamlining procurement and improving transparency, though digital penetration in B2B construction materials remains low (~1%). Market growth is fueled by rising infrastructure development and government policies supporting MSMEs and digitization.

Key Highlights

  • Operates asset-light, technology-driven B2B procurement model across 18 Indian states
  • Focus on sourcing and delivering multiple construction materials including RMC, aggregates, cement, steel, walling solutions, and chemicals
  • Plans to improve margins by increasing sale of third-party manufactured products
  • Extensive vendor network of 1,729 vendors serving 2,659 customers with deliveries in over 963 PIN codes
  • AI-powered document processing and logistics technologies such as 'ArisDelivery' and 'Cara AI' to automate operations
  • IPO fresh issue proceeds to partly repay short-term borrowings and fund working capital
  • Rated 'AVOID' due to expensive valuation at 48.3x FY25 annualized EV/EBITDA

Growth Strategies

  • Diversify construction material offerings to include high-margin products
  • Increase wallet share with existing customers leveraging key relationships
  • Expand strategic partnerships with third-party manufacturers for quality and margin improvement
  • Increase demand-side growth by partnering with key real estate and infrastructure players
  • Expand into new micro-markets geographically
  • Optimize working capital by improving payment and vendor terms
  • Leverage AI and technology to automate demand-supply syndication and improve operational efficiencies

Risks

  • Significant working capital needs and potential difficulty in securing financing on favorable terms
  • Possible disruptions in technology platforms impacting operations and financials
  • Cybersecurity risks leading to data breaches and reputational damage
  • High competition in digital construction material procurement space possibly affecting growth and margins
  • Seasonality in construction materials industry affecting revenue stability
  • Conflict of interest due to promoter's interests in similar businesses potentially impacting company operations

Valuation

ArisInfra's IPO priced at 48.3x annualized FY25 EV/EBITDA which appears expensive compared to peers. The company trades at 210.9x P/E while domestic and global peers trade between 9.0x-32.5x P/E. Recommend 'AVOID' due to high valuation and thin margins.

Other Notes

Listing date set for June 25, 2025; Leading book running managers are IIFL Capital Services, JM Financial, and Nuvama Wealth Management. The company operates in 18 Indian states and delivers to over 963 PIN codes, showing a wide geographic footprint with significant volumes delivered (14.10 million metric tonnes).
